Things I've written

I do like writing, but I rarely spend the time to organise my thoughts into something sufficiently coherent. On this page you'll find references to some of the things I wrote when the words flowed more freely (or when I was trying to excrete more content).

One thing to know about writing is that it can be something of a time capsule, so - even with an unchanged political outlook - the writing referenced here might not quite match my current thoughts and feelings.
